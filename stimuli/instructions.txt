Welcome to Dino Dash! 
Let me set the scene...
In this game you are playing as Dino the Dinosaur.
Your Goal is to escape Jusrasic Park, making your way through multiple obstacles.
To avoid the obstacles press your space bar.
Throughout the game there will be various power-ups and power-downs that will automatically enable to help or hurt you. 
Good Luck!
